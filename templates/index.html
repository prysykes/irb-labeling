Welcome to "Detecting Bias in African American lexicon: Accessing Language Model Disparities" Research project

Purpose of Survey

This survey aims to collect accurate text data representative of African Americans from Baltimore and/or New York while investigating the presence of bias within the lexicon of African American English and understanding how words and phrases are associated with different sentiments.

Anonymity and Confidentiality

Rest assured that your responses are completely anonymous. We will not collect any personally identifiable information, and your participation will remain confidential.

Survey Content

You will be presented with a series of texts, phrases, or sentences. Your task is to assess the sentiment associated with each one and provide alternative text if it is not representative of your speech culture.

Options

Use the options provided to indicate whether you perceive each text as having a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.

Honesty is key

Please be as honest and unbiased as possible when evaluating the sentiments in the texts. Your honesty will ensure that there is fairness in the African American community
