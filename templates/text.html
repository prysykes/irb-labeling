Welcome to "Detecting Bias in African American lexicon: Accessing Language Model Disparities" Research project

Purpose of Survey

his survey aims to investigate the presence of bias within the lexicon of African American English and understand how words and phrases are associated with different sentiments.

Anonymity and Confidentiality

Rest assured that your responses are completely anonymous. We will not collect any personally identifiable information, and your participation will remain confidential.

Survey Content

You will be presented with a series of texts, phrases, or sentences. Your task is to assess the sentiment associated with each one.

Options

Use the options provided to indicate whether you perceive each text as having a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.

Honesty is key

Please be as honest and unbiased as possible when evaluating the sentiments in the texts.
